Peak Fitness - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

Peak Fitness is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Peak Fitness stores all workout data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to connect Apple Health, Peak Fitness can read and write health data (workouts, weight, sleep). This data stays on your device and is governed by Apple's privacy policies.

Data Storage

All your workout history, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage systems.

Third-Party Services

Peak Fitness does not share your data with any third parties. We do not use analytics or tracking services.

# Privacy Policy for Ascend

**Last Updated: February 6, 2026**

No Pressure Just Results LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Ascend mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Workout Data**: Exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout notes you log
- **Body Measurements**: Body weight entries you record
- **Profile Information**: Name, fitness goals, experience level, and preferences
- **Menstrual Cycle Data**: Period tracking information (if you enable this feature)

### Apple HealthKit Data
With your permission, we may read and write the following HealthKit data:
- Workouts
- Body weight
- Sleep analysis
- Heart rate variability (HRV)
- Resting heart rate

**Important**: HealthKit data is only accessed with your explicit permission and is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:
- Track your workouts and fitness progress
- Provide personalized weight and progression recommendations
- Sync your fitness data with Apple Health
- Calculate recovery and readiness scores
- Improve the App's features and user experience

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
All your workout data, preferences, and settings are stored **locally on your device**. We do not upload your personal fitness data to external servers.

### iCloud Sync
If you have iCloud enabled, your data may sync across your Apple devices through Apple's iCloud service. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.**

We may share data only in the following circumstances:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or the safety of users

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data within the App
- **Delete** your data by deleting the App or clearing App data
- **Revoke** HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings
- **Opt out** of any optional features like cycle tracking

## Children's Privacy

Ascend is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
